Noodlarianism

(Novella)
Inspired by noodles, an anonymous emperor created an ideology and initiated its memetic evolution. The book contains a collection of chronological events and selective conversations from some crucial players that shape their alternative world. Some descriptions in the book may seemingly coincide with the actual figures, entities, and events, especially those staged in Eurasia (China in particular). However, readers should not confuse this imaginary world with the real one.

Culchemy's
Conclaves

(Novel)
Discover an alternative way of reading alchemical texts with this fascinating book. Inside, you'll find a collection of meeting memos from a secret society, Culchemy. Their current mission? To upend the perennial ideology of Noodlarianism, another secretive group that controls the fabricated central plain by continuously stirring the forces of "noodles" and "gravy." As the board of Culchemy proposes various strategies for embarking on a culchemical journey, they reveal a breathtaking spiritual adventure that will leave you wanting more. Dive into the world of Culchemy and experience a different perspective on a spiritual journey like never before.
