Shujie Yang

Mer

Biography


Mer graduated from the China Academy of Art graduate. Currently, she resides in Jingdezhen, where she passionately engages in ceramics, fibers, painting, and photography. Her artistic endeavors revolve around exploring the lost Feminine energy and the innocence of the childlike spirit, transcending civilizations, histories, and spiritual legacies. With a keen focus on the interconnectedness of the past and present, as well as the interplay between the personal and collective realms, she weaves a captivating tapestry of artistry.

Drawing inspiration from diverse regional cultural narratives and folk imagery, she masterfully reinterprets them through her artistic lens, presenting a visually rich and harmonious world. Her innovative blending of various elements yields a remarkable synthesis of unity and diversity. Each artwork she creates becomes a vessel for storytelling, imbued with both personal and universal narratives.

By dedicating herself to the meticulous craftsmanship of textiles, ceramics, and decorative arts and her profound fascination with the human form, she delves into themes of desire, life, energy, and joy, employing a range of artistic media across different series of works. Through her creative expressions, she embraces art as a vibrant celebration of life, capturing the intricate beauty and complexities that define our world.

个人简介


毕业于中国美术学院雕塑专业, 如今常驻景德镇, 她以多种媒介进行艺术探索，包括陶瓷、纤维、绘画、摄影。致⼒于在多种⽂明历史与精神遗产之中，追溯失落的 ⼥性力量与孩童精神，透过创作探索过去与现在、个⼈与集体性的关系。在她的作品之中，她以⾃己的⽅式融合诸多地域⽂化故事和⺠间图像，呈现⼀个混杂奇异且充满生机的视觉世界。

她混合不同元素的特殊⽅式同时创造了统⼀感和多样性，每个细节讲述了一个故事，这种叙述同时是个人的也是宇宙的。

通过对纺织品、陶瓷、装饰物等⼿工艺的持续关注和实践，以及对身体的兴趣， 她藉由不同的媒介释放与情欲、⽣命、能量 、喜悦相关的主题。她将⾃己的创作视为一场⽣命的庆典，映射着我们所⽣活的世界的美丽和复杂性。
